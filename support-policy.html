Support Policy

This policy explains how EduGo handles learner support requests and platform assistance.

How to contact support

You can contact EduGo support by using the contact page or emailing our support team. Please include your name, account email, course name, and a clear description of the issue so we can help efficiently.

Support hours

Support requests are typically reviewed during regular business hours. Response times may vary based on request volume, issue complexity, holidays, or service interruptions.

What we can help with

  • Account access and password reset questions.
  • Course enrollment and access issues.
  • Basic billing or purchase questions for live services.
  • Technical issues related to using EduGo pages and learning materials.

What is outside support scope

EduGo support does not provide personalized career counseling, completion of assignments on behalf of learners, third-party software troubleshooting, or support for devices and networks outside the platform.

Issue resolution

We may ask for additional details, screenshots, browser information, or steps to reproduce a problem. Some issues may require investigation or follow-up after the initial response.
